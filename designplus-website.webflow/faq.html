Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely no hidden fees. The package price is exactly what you pay on a monthly basis. All taxes and fees are included in that price.

Am I locked into a long-term contract?

Not at all, you are enrolled on a month to month membership and can cancel anytime – hassle free.

How long is your turnaround time?

Our turn-around time depends on project and number of revisions. Usually it’s between 12 – 48 hours for most projects. More complex design project such as a website/app design, pitch decks, and multi-page booklets will require more time.

I run multiple businesses. Can I use 1 account?

Yes, as a matter of fact, we built our online software to be easy and intuitive for you to create and manage design project for multiple businesses and brands. You can use Design+ for yourself, your other businesses, or even your customers.

Do I own the rights to all my designs?

You own all rights and licenses to all designs created for you. If we use stock photos, you will also be granted license for that photo. However, materials you provide to us are assumed to be licensed and owned by you. If you provide us with unlicensed materials to use, then we are not held liable for the rights of the final design.

How many hours a month will my designer work?

With Design+ you never have to worry about hourly billing or unexpected charges. When we promise “Unlimited” we do mean it. We work via design projects. When one design project is complete, we move onto the next one on your queue that same day.

Are you able to completely design iOS/Android Apps?

Yes, we can completely design an iOS/Android mobile app. Projects of this scale will take time and require extensive reviews, revisions, and approvals for each page.

Do you make canceling difficult?

No. You don’t have to write us a letter, jump through a hoop of fire, or go through 15 pages to find the cancel button. Simply tell your project manager or email us at support@designplus.global. The only question we ask if “What could we have done better to keep you?”
